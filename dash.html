Wi-Fi Access

To access wifi, you will need to join one of the following.

EDUROAM Preferred: You must have already set up EduRoam at your school for it to work here.
UNC PSK For this network, you must have registered your mac address with us. IF you need to register, visit the Help Desk.
UNC Guest Limited access. Only has port 80 and 443 open. Bad for hacking.
