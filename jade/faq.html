FAQs

Can I attend?

High school over 18, undergraduate, and graduate students are welcome to hack. Professionals are welcome to join as mentors, sponsors, or lecturers.

Where is the app?

The HackNC App is available now for iOS, Android, and all other devices as a web app. Download today!

What about sleep?

Bring your own pillow and bedding. There will be a sleeping space available Friday and Saturday nights. If you'd prefer a hotel room, the Carolina Inn is only 200 feet away (although very expensive).

Travel costs?

See our travel policy for more information.

What is it?

A weekend of fun and learning. You'll become a better programmer, meet new people, and make cool stuff. You'll also have the chance to learn a lot and get loads of free stuff!

Can I use old code?

You may use all the publicly available code and APIs that you want but the code you will be judged on at the end of the hackathon must be new.

Hardware?

MLH is providing the full hardware lab. In addition we will have soldering and assorted components, Arduinos, Raspberry Pis, and more Pebbles than you can wear on four limbs...

How do I get in?

Anyone can attend HackNC. Just thoughtfully fill out the application. The only limitation is the amount of food we can serve over the weekend.

Will there be food?

Don’t worry. We’ll provide six full meals throughout the hackathon and there will be plenty of snacks in between. You won’t be hungry.

Admission decisions?

Admission decisions will be announced on August 22 for early admission and September 12th for regular admission. Afterwards, we will review applications on a case-by-case basis.

What about my team?

Be yourself; sign up as an individual! You can create your team with whoever you like, up to four people, once you arrive.

Will I fit in?

Yes, you will! HackNC and UNC Computer Science is committed to making computer science a more diverse field. We are working to make this the most inclusive HackNC yet.
